How It Works

Built for authentic real-world connections. Set your intentions, craft the perfect pitch, and find your next big connection nearby.

  • 1. Set Your Intentions: Tell the community exactly what you’re looking for today.
  • 2. Craft Your Pitch: Use our AI-assistant to generate the perfect introduction.
  • 3. Discover Nearby: Check the interactive map or real-time feed for active users.
  • 4. Bridge the Gap: Match, chat instantly, and meet up in person.

Privacy & Safety First

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities. Bridgit uses advanced privacy protection to keep you safe while connecting. Your real location is never shared—only approximate coordinates in Walking Distance mode.

  • Anonymous browsing until you match
  • Fuzzy location pins for Walking Distance privacy
  • Built-in AI moderation and reporting systems
  • Hide, report, or block users anytime
  • Granular privacy controls: You decide when you're visible
