How It Works

Simple, intuitive, and designed for real-world connections. Create your profile, choose your networking mode, and discover people around you who are ready to connect.

  • Real-time, location-based discovery
  • Anonymous browsing until you match
  • "Mark to Meet" for mutual interest
  • Privacy-protected map view
  • Private notes after connections
  • Two modes: Same Place or Walking Distance

Privacy & Safety First

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities. Bridgit uses advanced privacy protection to keep you safe while connecting. Your real location is never shared—only approximate coordinates in Walking Distance mode.

  • Actual GPS coordinates never shared
  • Fuzzy location pins for privacy
  • Hide, report, or block users anytime
  • Meeting confirmation & archiving tools
