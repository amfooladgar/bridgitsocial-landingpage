Welcome to Bridgit Social

Bridgit Social helps you discover and connect with real people, in real-time, wherever you are. Whether you're at a cafe, coworking space, or simply nearby—start conversations that matter.

Get Started

How It Works

Create your profile with a face photo, choose your networking mode (Same Place or Walking Distance), and go online. See who's around, express interest anonymously with "Mark to Meet," and connect when the interest is mutual. After meeting, confirm and record private notes.

  • 📍 Real-time, real-place connections
  • 🙈 Stay anonymous until matched
  • 📝 Private note-taking post-meeting
  • 🗺️ View users on a privacy-protected map

Privacy & Safety

Bridgit uses approximate coordinates in Walking Distance mode and only shows real presence in Same Place mode with permission. Your identity is only shared when mutual interest is confirmed. We never show your actual GPS coordinates to other users.

  • 🔒 No real GPS shared
  • 🌀 Fake map pins to mask location
  • 🚫 Hide profiles permanently
  • ✅ Meeting confirmation & archiving tools
