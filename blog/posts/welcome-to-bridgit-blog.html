What is Bridgit Social?

Bridgit Social reimagines professional networking for the modern era. We're not another swipe-left-swipe-right app. We're a tool designed for people who value authentic connections over endless scrolling.

The Problem We're Solving

Let's be honest: traditional networking is broken.

  • LinkedIn is great for passive connections but terrible for meeting people in real-time
  • Conferences feel like speed-dating marathons where you collect cards you'll never use
  • Random coffee shop encounters happen by pure luck, not by design
  • Existing social apps prioritize superficial swiping over meaningful conversations

The result? Professionals spend hours "networking" without building real relationships.

Our Solution: Location + Intention + Privacy

Bridgit Social combines three powerful elements:

  1. Location Intelligence: Know who's nearby and available right now
  2. Intentional Matching: Connect with people who actually want to meet
  3. Privacy-First Design: Your exact location and personal data stay protected

How Bridgit Actually Works

Two Powerful Modes

🟢 Same Place Mode

Perfect for:

  • Working from a coffee shop and wondering who else is building something interesting
  • Attending a conference and wanting to find attendees you'd click with
  • Co-working spaces where you want to network without awkward cold approaches
  • Events where you want to connect before, during, or after programming

When you're in the same physical location, Bridgit helps you discover fellow attendees, workers, or event-goers who are open to connecting—no guesswork required.

🚶 Walking Distance Mode

Ideal for:

  • Finding professionals nearby for spontaneous coffee meetups
  • Discovering people in you neighborhood you'd never meet otherwise
  • Networking while traveling (find locals or fellow visitors)
  • Building community in your city

Your exact location stays private. Others see only approximate pins showing you're within walking distance, preserving privacy while enabling connection.

The Mark to Meet Feature

This is where Bridgit shines. Instead of swiping on strangers:

  1. Browse nearby profiles with interests, background, and what they're working on
  2. Mark to Meet people who genuinely interest you, adding a personalized note explaining why
  3. Match when the feeling is mutual and start a real conversation
  4. Meet up in person when both people are actually available

No ghosting. No endless messaging. Just meaningful connections with people who want to meet you too.

Privacy Protections You Can Trust

We take your safety seriously:

  • Fuzzy Location: Walking Distance mode never shares your exact coordinates
  • Anonymous Browsing: Explore profiles privately until you match
  • Quick Actions: Hide, report, or block anyone instantly
  • Meeting Confirmation: Track who you've met for accountability
  • Data Minimization: We collect only what's necessary for the app to work

What Makes Bridgit Different

1. Real-Time Focus

Other apps optimize for browsing. We optimize for meeting. When you're on Bridgit, you're signaling: "I'm available to connect right now."

2. No Superficial Swiping

We removed the dopamine-driven swipe mechanics. Instead, you intentionally Mark to Meet people, explaining why you want to connect. It takes more effort, but creates infinitely better matches.

3. Privacy by Design

We built privacy protections from day one, not as an afterthought. Your data is yours. We don't sell it, we don't exploit it, we don't share it without permission.

4. Meeting Accountability

The "Met In-Person" feature creates accountability. Confirming meetings builds trust in the community and discourages flaking.

5. Platform for Authentic Networking

Unlike social apps designed for addiction, Bridgit is designed for utility. Get in, make connections, meet people, get back to living your life.

Getting Started: Your First Week on Bridgit

Day 1: Set Up Your Profile

  • Upload a clear, recent photo of your face
  • Write a concise bio (focus on what you're working on and seeking)
  • List 3-5 genuine interests or expertise areas
  • Set your networking goals (collaboration, learning, career, etc.)

Pro Tip: Specific beats generic. "Building a climate tech marketplace" is better than "entrepreneur."

Day 2-3: Explore Your Mode

Try both modes to see what works for your lifestyle:

  • Coffee shop worker? → Same Place mode
  • Want to expand your local network? → Walking Distance mode

Day 4-5: Make Your First Connections

  • Browse profiles thoughtfully
  • Mark to Meet 3-5 people with personalized notes
  • When you match, propose a specific time and place
  • Actually show up (seriously, don't flake)

Day 6-7: Reflect and Iterate

  • What types of connections energized you?
  • What settings work best (cafes, coworking, events)?
  • How can you refine your profile and approach?
